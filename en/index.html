About Us

IP República Bolivariana de Venezuela

The IP República Bolivariana de Venezuela is an educational center dedicated to training technical professionals, committed to academic excellence and the comprehensive development of its students. Our center is located in Consejo Popular No 3, Güines, Mayabeque.

Our Facilities

Our school is located in Consejo Popular No. 3, Circunscription No. 1, on Calle Km 3 1/2, Río Seco, Güines municipality, Mayabeque province.

Block 1: Academic

13 classrooms, 2 computer labs, school library, history room, drawing classroom.

Block 2: Sports & Dining

Basketball and volleyball courts, baseball area, dining hall for 150 students, 8 dormitories (3 operational).
